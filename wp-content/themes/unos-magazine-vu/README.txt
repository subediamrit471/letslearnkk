== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to install Unos Magazine Vu =

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in 'Unos Magazine Vu' in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.

= How to get theme support =

You can look at the theme instructions at https://wphoot.com/support/unos-magazine-vu/ To get support beyond the scope of documentation provided, please open a support ticket via https://wphoot.com/support/
